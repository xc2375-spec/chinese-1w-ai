Unit 16: Reading Fluency

Listen, shadow, and repeat five formal sentences related to environment, education, and fairness.

How to do shadow reading

First listen to the model audio. Then read aloud together with the recording. Finally, try reading the sentence again on your own.

The goal here is not speed. The goal is clear rhythm, natural phrasing, and confident expression.
Listen Read Along Shadow Repeat

Fluency Tips

Try not to stop after every word. Read in meaning groups. Pay attention to longer phrases like “教育机会应该更加公平” and “孩子成长的环境会影响他们的学习习惯”.
